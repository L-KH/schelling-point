The Conference

Schelling Point is one day celebration of coordination, public goods, and regenerative finance.

The goal of this event is to be a “schelling point for the hopeful”; our core audience is people who want to use Ethereum to build a better, more coordinated, world (even if they dont know it yet)

By co-creating this schelling point with you, we hope to help people to reconnect with why we're actually here in this space (Ethereum is about being free, not just rich) & accelerate the narrative of coordination technology, regenerative economic experiments, Solarpunk lore, and the fruits of public goods.
